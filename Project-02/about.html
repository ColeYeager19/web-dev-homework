Why Choose to Teach Cooking?

My mother is the best chef I've ever known and to this day remains entirely self taught. As I grew up she taught me everything she knew about cooking and baking so that I would both be able to assist her, but also so I'd know the family recipes by heart. Looking back my favorite memories are of her teaching me to cook, so I decided I should pass some of that experience along. Since salmon is her favorite dish I found a couple recipes from the video and website that I thought may be worth sharing. My hope is that the ease of these recipes incourages others to learn so they can share the same appreciation of cooking that I now hold.


If you would like to learn more recipes you can reach out to me via the contact page!
